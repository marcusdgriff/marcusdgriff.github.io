Biography

I am a postdoctoral fellow working on root functional phenomics.

My current position is at the Noble Research Institute under the direction of Dr. Larry M York. I am working on a NIFA/DOE project to phenotype multiple ion uptake kinetics in maize.

Before moving to the US I did my PhD at The Centre For Plant Integrative Biology at the Univeristy of Nottingham. I used hydroponics and X-ray CT to phenotype root system architecuture in wheat under the direction of Dr. Darren M Wells and Prof. Malcolm J Bennett.

In addition, I have been recently accepted onto the Associate Reviewer Board for the Plant Direct journal.


Research interests
  • Plant physiology
  • Root phenomics
  • Nutrient uptake kinetics
  • R statistics
